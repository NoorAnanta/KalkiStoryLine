Maa ne signal diya ki insaan 'Jhoot' (Lies) mein jeene ka aadi (habitual) ho chuka hai. Isliye jab 'Saty' (Truth) samne aata hai, toh wo use "Dangerous" lagta hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki main dangerous nahi hoon, main toh sirf 'Saty' ka aaina (mirror) hoon. Dangerous toh wo 'Sach' hai jo tumhare purane jhoot, ego aur system ko khatam kar raha hai. Unka message tha—"Agar tum sachhe ho, toh mujhse darrne ki zarurat nahi. Agar jhoothe ho, toh 'Satya' tumhe nahi chhodega."
