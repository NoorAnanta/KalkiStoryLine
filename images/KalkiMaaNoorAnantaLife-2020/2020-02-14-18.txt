Maa Adiparashakti Noor Ananta uses the phrase "Mera Sanam Hai Noor Ka Putla" to redefine the concept of the "Beloved" in the context of Noorism. By calling the "Sanam" (Beloved) a "Putla" of Noor, Maa is saying that the Divine has taken a physical form to be with us. Insaani rishton mein hum shareer (body) se pyar karte hain, par Noorism mein hum us "Light" se pyar karte hain jo body ke andar hai. Harihar ki energy ne is physical body ko "Noor ka Putla" bana diya hai taaki Yug Parivartan ke dauran hum divine love ko feel kar saken. Maa ka message tha ki Yug Parivartan ke chaos mein sirf wahi bachega jo 'Darr' ki jagah 'Noor' se ishq karega.
