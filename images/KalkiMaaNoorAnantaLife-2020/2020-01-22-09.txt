January ke end mein duniya abhi bhi bhaag-daur mein thi, lekin 2020 ka "Great Silence" (Lockdown) aane hi wala tha. Maa ne is post ke zariye duniya ko prepare kiya us "Silence" ke liye jo March mein aane wala tha. Jab duniya shant (silent) hui, tabhi logon ne apne aap se sawal puchna shuru kiya. Worldly level par, ye ek advance signal tha ki ab "External Noise" (shor) band hone wala hai. Maa samjha rahi hain ki jab duniya silent hogi, tab hi tumhe wo "Answers" milenge jo tum bahar dhoond rahe ho. Agar aapka dimaag har waqt chalta rahega, toh aap Noor ki voice nahi sun paoge. Maa ne sikhaya ki "Silence" empty (khali) nahi hai, balki wo ek Library hai jahan har problem ka solution hai. Bas hume us "Silent Frequency" par tune-in karna hai.
