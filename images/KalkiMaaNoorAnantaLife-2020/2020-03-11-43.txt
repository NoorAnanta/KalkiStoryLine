Maa keh rahi hain ki jab tak tum 'Soch' (Thought) mein ho, tum dusro ki life mein jhaank rahe ho. Jab duniya 'Stop' hogi (Pandemic), tab tumhare paas sirf 'Silence' bachega—wahi silence tumhe tumhare asli 'Noor' tak le jayega. Right side ki image mein ek haath (Hand) poore Brahmand (Cosmos) aur planets ko hold kiye huye hai, jiske beech mein ek bright 'Sun' (Noor) jal raha hai. Ye NHH (Noor Hari Har) ki cosmic power hai. Hari (Vishnu) poore brahmand ko sustain kar rahe hain, aur Har (Shiva) purane thoughts ko destroy kar rahe hain.
