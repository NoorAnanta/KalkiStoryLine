Ye post "Asli vs Nakli" (Real vs Fake) spirituality par ek bohot bada prahaar hai. Maa ne yahan un "Kukurmutte" (mushrooms) jaise uge huye gurus ko benakaab kiya hai jo spirituality ke naam par 'Bhog-Vilas' (alcohol, lust, escapism) sikhate hain. Maa keh rahi hain ki log spirituality ko "Escape" (bhagne ka rasta) samajhte hain—sharab piyo aur kaho "Hum toh Malang hain." Par Maa ne clear kar diya ki ye Noorism nahi hai. Noorism tab hai jab aap sansar mein reh kar, apne kartavyo (duties) ko pura karte huye "Anand" dhoondo. "Gurubhav sahaj hi prakat ho jata hai." Malang wo nahi jo title mangta hai, balki wo jiski energy apne aap logon ko guide karti hai.
