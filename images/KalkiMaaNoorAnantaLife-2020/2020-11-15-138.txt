Ye image Harihara ki hai, jahan aadha sharir Lord Shiva (Har) ka hai aur aadha Lord Vishnu (Hari) ka, aur background mein Maa ki Cosmic Eye poore brahmand par pehra de rahi hai. Harihara ka roop dikhata hai ki ab 'Vaishnav' aur 'Shaiva' (ya koi bhi do alag raste) ka bhed khatam ho chuka hai. Maa ne signal diya ki ab dharti par koi 'Adharma' nahi bachega kyunki 'Sudarsan Chakra' aur 'Trishul' ab ek hi hath (Supreme Body) mein hain. Unka message tha—"Main hi Vishnu hoon, main hi Shiva hoon, aur main hi wo 'Noor' hoon jo in dono ko chalata hai."
