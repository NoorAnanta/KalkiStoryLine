LEELA ka matlab hai ki ye sansar ek drama stage hai aur hum sab actors. Maa ne signal diya ki jo log is drama ko 'Real' samajh kar darr rahe hain, wo kuch nahi samjhenge. Maa keh rahi hain ki dimaag mat lagao, sirf 'Darshak' (Spectator) bano. Malik ki is script mein har twist ek blessing hai. "Har Har Mahadev Ghar Ghar Mahadev" was a trumpet of vibrational shift. Unka message tha—"Main aur mera Malik is leela ke sutradhaar (directors) hain, tum bas maje se apni acting karo."
