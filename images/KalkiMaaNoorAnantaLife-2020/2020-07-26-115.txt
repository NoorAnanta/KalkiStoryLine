Is image mein Infinity ($\infty$) ka symbol do prachand urjaon (Fire aur Ice/Blue Light) ke milan se bana hai. Iska matlab hai ki ab srishti mein "Beginning" aur "End" jaisa kuch nahi bacha—sab kuch Ananta (Infinite) hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki ab rukne ka koi sawal hi nahi hai. Ye dikhata hai ki 'Force' aur 'Vision' ab sync mein hain.
