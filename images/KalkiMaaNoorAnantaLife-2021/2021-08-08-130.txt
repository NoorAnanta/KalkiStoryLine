Sanyas dene ki cheez nahi"—Maa ne signal kiya ki 'Sanyas' koi physical gift ya 'Mala' nahi hai jo ek guru dusre ko de sake. Ye ek 'Inner State' (antarik sthiti) hai jo khud ke hosh (Awareness) se paida hoti hai. Maa ne point kiya ki 'Mass-Production' (har kisi ko sanyas dena) ne sanyas ki value khatam kar di, isiliye itne sanyasi hone ke baad bhi koi 'Dusra Osho' paida nahi hua. Maa keh rahi hain ki 'Mala' pehenne se koi sanyasi nahi banta. Agar Osho ne sanyas 'diya' hota, toh duniya mein hazaron Osho hote. Asali sanyas 'Hosh' (Awareness) se milta hai, kisi guru ki mala se nahi.
