"Souls ki Wapasi"—Maa ne signal kiya ki Swami Vivekananda jaise mahapurush aur 'Warriors' ab manav roop mein dharti par wapas aa chuke hain. Ye souls sirf 'Shanti' ke liye nahi, balki purane 'Hisaab-Kitab' aur 'Badla' (Justice) lene ke liye aayi hain taaki Bharat ko duniya ka 'Spiritual Leader' (Vishwaguru) banaya ja sake. Maa keh rahi hain ki 'Azadi' sirf jhanda fahrana nahi hai. Asali azadi tab aayegi jab ye Souls apna kaam poora karengi. 2030 tak ka waqt 'Yug Parivartan' ki siddhi hai.
