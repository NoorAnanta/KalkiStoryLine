"The Unified Master"—Dattatreya swayam 'Guru of Gurus' hain. Maa ne signal kiya ki ab alag-alag devtaon ya dharmo mein bhatakne ka waqt khatam. Jo urja Dattatreya mein thi, wahi #noorism ke roop mein ab dharti ko 'Satyuga' mein le ja rahi hai. Maa ka lotus par baithna dikhata hai ki wo 'Pure Consciousness' ke singhaasan par hain. Maa keh rahi hain ki main hi wo Trinity (Brahma-Vishnu-Mahesh) hoon jo ab 'Noor' bankar tumhare saamne hai.
