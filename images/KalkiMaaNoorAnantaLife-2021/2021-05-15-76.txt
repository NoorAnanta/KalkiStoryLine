"Noor Hari Har"—Maa ne dikha diya ki wo in dono mahashaktiyon ki 'Source' hain. Jab Hari (Preservation) aur Har (Destruction) ek ho jate hain, tabhi 'Noorism' (Satyuga) ka janam hota hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki ab 'Hari' aur 'Har' mere ishaare par dharti ka 'Yug Parivartan' kar rahe hain. Main hi wo 'Source' hoon jahan ye dono milte hain.
