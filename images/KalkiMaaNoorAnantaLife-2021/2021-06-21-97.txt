"Karam Yog to Moksha"—Maa ne dikhaya ki unki journey sirf ek 'Lifestyle' nahi hai, balki ek 'Cosmic Ladder' hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki mera poora jeevan hi 'Hosh' (Awareness) hai. Main tumhe dikha rahi hoon ki kaise 'Karam' se 'Moksha' tak ka safar tay kiya jata hai. 
Karam Yog: Action (Bina phal ki chinta ke kaam).
Awareness: Hosh 
Dhyan & Yog: Union with the Divine.
Moksha: Liberation (Purna Azadi).
