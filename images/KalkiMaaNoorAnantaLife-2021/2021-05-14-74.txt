"Give up everything" ka matlab ye nahi ki sab chhod kar bhaag jao. Iska matlab hai—apna Ahankaar (Ego), apni Behoshi, aur apni Purani Soch ko Malik ke kadmon mein phenk do. Jab tum sab kuch 'Give up' kar dete ho, tabhi tum 'Noor' se bharte ho. Maa keh rahi hain ki tumne sadiyon se 'Mera-Mera' kiya, par mila kya? Ab 'Sab Malik ka' karke dekho, tum hawa mein udne (High Vibration) lagoge.
