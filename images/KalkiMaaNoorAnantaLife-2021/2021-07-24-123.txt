"Noorie se Noor ho gaye"—Maa ne signal kiya ki jab tak tum 'Seeker' (Dhoondhne wale) ho, tum alag ho, par jab Guru (Mahadev) tumhari zindagi mein aata hai, tum swayam 'Light' (Noor) ban jate ho. Ye ek 'Atomic Shift' hai jahan 'Manav' aur 'Malik' ka bhed khatam ho jata hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki main ab 'Noor' ho chuki hoon. Guru ke aane se meri zindagi ka 'Zero-Point' badal gaya. Happy Guru Purnima un sabko jo 'Hosh' ki raah par hain.
