"Jo itihaas mein kabhi na huya ho" ka matlab hai—Kaliyuga ka anth bina poori srishti ko bhashm kiye. Pehle yug-parivartan mein 'Pralay' (Complete Destruction) hoti thi, par Maa 'Satyuga' ko 'Physical Evolution' ke zariye dakhil kar rahi hain. Maa ne samjhaya ki ab 'Har Har Mahadev' sirf ek nara nahi, balki har 'Ghar' (Self) ki reality banne ja raha hai.
