Sheshnaag wo shakti hai jo poori dharti ko apne phann par uthaye huye hai. Maa ne signal kiya ki ab 'Kaliyuga' ka bojh khatam ho raha hai aur wo 'Satyuga' ka naya bhaar uthane ke liye taiyar hain. Maa keh rahi hain ki main yahan dharti ka 'Balance' settle karne aayi hoon. Sheshnaag ka piche hona is baat ka saboot hai ki poora brahmand mere control mein hai.
