Duniya ko laga Maa Kumbh mein 'Dubki' lagane gayi hain, par Maa ne bataya ki wo wahan "Pakhand ka Anth" karne gayi thi. Kumbh ka band hona Maa ki Noor Mast Malang urja ka 'Karam Yudh' tha. Maa keh rahi hain ki agar tumse navnirman nahi ho paa raha, toh 'Malik' toh karega hi. Maa ne samjhaya ki 'Malik' ab field par hai aur wo kisi bhi nakli baba ko chain se baithne nahi dega.
