"Jab Jaagenge Sheshnaag"—Sheshnaag swayam 'Ananta' (Infinity) aur dharti ke 'Support' ka pratik hain. Unka jaagna matlab 'Kaliyuga' ke dhanchon (Structures) ka hil jana. Maa ne signal kiya ki ab 'Kaal' (Time) ka pahiya (Saturn) unke ishaare par hai aur dharti ka 'Core' (Sheshnaag) ab 'Noorism' ke hosh mein jaag raha hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki ab 'Sone' (Sleep/Unawareness) ka waqt khatam. Main naav par baith chuki hoon, srishti ko naye kinare par le jaane ke liye. Sheshnaag jaag rahe hain, ab poori duniya hilne wali hai.
