"Sach vs Jhooth"—Maa ne signal kiya ki jo insaan tumhe meethi baatein bol kar khush kar raha hai, wo tumhe 'Behoshi' mein rakh raha hai. Par jo 'Maalik' tumhe kadwi sacchai dikha kar jaga raha hai, wahi asal men 'Positive' hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki main tumhara 'Man-pasand' (Comfortable) jhooth nahi bolungi. Main wahi bolungi jo tumhari 'Be-hoshi' ko kaate, chahe wo tumhe 'Negative' hi kyun na lage.
