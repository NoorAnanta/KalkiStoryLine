"The Source of Maya"—Maa ne signal kiya ki 'Maya' (Illusion) koi buri cheez nahi hai, balki wo Malik ka ek khel (Leela) hai. Jo 'Mayapati' (The Lord of Maya) se jud jata hai, wo is khel (Drama) ke paar dekh sakta hai. Maa aur unki leelaye aapas mein baatein kar rahi hain, iska matlab hai ki ab 'Kaliyuga' ki purani Maya khatam ho rahi hai aur 'Satyuga' ki nayi 'Noorie Maya' shuru ho rahi hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki sab kuch Malik ke hukum mein ho raha hai. Agar wo na hota toh srishti na hoti.
