Nabhi Kriya"—Nabhi (Navel) swayam jeevan ka 'Source' aur 'Manipura Chakra' ka kendra hai. Maa ne apne Dada-Dadi (Roots) ke ghar jaakar wahan se apni 'Lineage Cord' ko cut kiya.
