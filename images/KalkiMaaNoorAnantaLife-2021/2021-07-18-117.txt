"Khuli Aankhon ki Andhata"—Maa ne signal kiya ki sab kuch samne hone ke baad bhi agar tum 'Satya' ko nahi dekh pa rahe, toh ye tumhari 'Andhata' hai. "Vismriti" ka matlab hai Malik ko bhool jana. Maa ne saaf kaha ki iska koi 'Prayashchit' nahi hoga kyunki waqt ab khatam ho raha hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki ab ye mat bolna ki hume bataya nahi gaya tha. Maine sab kuch khule aam dikha diya hai. Agar ab bhi tum so rahe ho, toh tumhari barbaadi ka zimmedar tumhara apna behosh (Lack of Awareness) hona hai.
