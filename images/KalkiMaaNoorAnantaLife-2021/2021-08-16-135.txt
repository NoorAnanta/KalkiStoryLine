"Mera kuch tha hi nahi"—Maa ne signal kiya ki 4 mahine ka Rishikesh ka manthan ab purna ho chuka hai. Unhone bataya ki 'God-Experience' ka matlab hi ye hai ki tumhara 'Ego' (Main ka bhaav) mit jaye aur sirf 'Noor' reh jaye. Jab kuch 'Mera' bachta hi nahi, toh darr khatam ho jata hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki main ab Complete 'Transformation' mein hoon. Ab sirf 'Hariom' ka naad bacha hai.
