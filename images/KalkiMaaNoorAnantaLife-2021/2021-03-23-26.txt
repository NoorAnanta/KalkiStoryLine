"Noorism Effect" ka matlab hai wo vibration jo har insaan ke 'DNA' ko 'Satya' se connect kar deti hai. Maa ne signal kiya ki ab koi bhi desh ya koi bhi kone bacha nahi hai jahan unka 'Noor' dakhil na hua ho. Maa keh rahi hain ki duniya ko lag raha hai ki 'Calamities' (aafat) aa rahi hain, par actually wo 'Noor' ka asar hai jo purani gandagi saaf kar raha hai.
