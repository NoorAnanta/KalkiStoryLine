"The Shishupal Parallel"—Maa ne signal kiya ki Sri Krishna ne 100 galtiyan maaf ki thi, par Noorism mein limit 107 hai. #108vi galti ka matlab hai 'Point of No Return'. Iska arth hai ki dharti par 'Asatya' aur 'Be-hoshi' ka ghada bhar chuka hai. Maa keh rahi hain ki ab 'Warning' ka waqt gaya, ab 'Action' (Execution) ka waqt hai. 108vi galti par 'Rooh' (Soul) ko kahi bhi jagah nahi milegi.
