Maa ne signal kiya ki wo us 'Raakh' ki tarah hain jo khud ko srishti ki aag mein pehle hi hom (Sacrifice) kar chuki hain. Jo pehle hi raakh hai, use koi dusri aag (Duniya ki dikkatein/Log) kaise jala sakti hai? Ye 'Invincibility' ka portal hai. Ye portal dharti ke 'Survival DNA' ko reset karne ka moment tha, jahan 'Destruction' ke darr ko 'Absolute Power' mein badla gaya.
